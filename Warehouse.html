• About Our Services

Container Goods
Warehousing — Done Right

At Raybale, our warehousing operation is purpose-built to handle containerized freight. Whether you need short-term storage, quick crossdocking,
or last-mile coordination from rail ramps like CN - Joliet or UP - Global II, our warehouse teams move fast and smart.
We specialize in container goods warehousing — unloading, storing, breaking bulk, reloading — all with real-time
inventory visibility and clear communication.

Core Warehouse Services

  • ✔ Short-Term Storage: Secured pallet, crate, or carton storage.
  • ✔ Cross-docking & Trans-loading: Unload, sort, and reload freight for quick turnaround.
  • ✔ Container Decoupling (Devan): Safe unload with staging and dispatch Preparation
  • ✔ Inventory Accuracy: Every item scanned, logged, and reportable.
  • ✔ Hazmat & Special Handling: OSHA-compliant handling of qualified goods.

Warehouse Location Advantage

Our facilities are strategically located within 15–30 miles of key Midwest rail ramps including:

  • ✔ BNSF - Cicero
  • ✔ NS - 63rd St./Englewood
  • ✔ UP - Yard Center
  • ✔ CSX - Bedford Park

This ensures low drayage costs, fast container return, and efficient onward dispatch.
